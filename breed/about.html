Who We Are

Horse Loverz offers practical, real-life tips and training advice to help you care for your horse. Our website covers a variety of topics, like horse breeds. Our team of writers, including trainers, Registered Vet Technicians (RVTs) and Doctors of Veterinary Medicine (DVMs), produce and update informative articles full of valuable insights honed from years of hands-on experience.
